About MOVR 2.0

What is MOVR 2.0? +

MOVR 2.0 is the next generation neuromuscular disease registry platform that moves from manual data entry to automated EMR integration while maintaining gold standard data quality and building open source tools for the entire research community.

How is MOVR 2.0 different from the original MOVR? +

While the original MOVR required manual data entry from clinical staff, MOVR 2.0 features:

  • Automated data collection through EMR integration
  • Real-time processing and validation
  • Open source tools for the research community
  • Reduced site burden while maintaining data quality

For Previous MOVR Participants

I was enrolled in the original MOVR. What happened to my data? +

Your participation in the original MOVR has concluded, and your consent has been withdrawn as planned. However, the data you contributed continues to support important neuromuscular disease research through approved studies and publications.

Can I get a copy of my MOVR data? +

Yes! You can request your historical MOVR data through our Legacy Data Request Form. We'll provide you with a summary of the data you contributed to the registry.

Can I participate in MOVR 2.0? +

Absolutely! We'd love to have you join us in the next chapter. MOVR 2.0 enrollment will be opening soon. Send us an email at MDAMOVR@mdausa.org to express your interest, and we'll keep you posted as soon as registration opens.

For Researchers

Can I still access original MOVR data for research? +

Legacy MOVR data (USNDR 2013-2018 and MOVR 2019-2025) remains available for approved research studies. Contact us at MDAMOVR@mdausa.org to discuss data access for your research project.

How can I get involved with MOVR 2.0 development? +

We're building MOVR 2.0 with the research community. You can:

  • Join our beta program to test new tools
  • Contribute to open source development
  • Partner with us for EMR integration pilots
  • Provide feedback on platform features

Email us at MDAMOVR@mdausa.org to learn more about these opportunities.

Technical Questions

What EMR systems will MOVR 2.0 support? +

We're currently in the operational validation phase, testing integration with major EMR systems. Our goal is to support the most widely used systems in neuromuscular care, prioritizing those used by MDA Care Centers and other specialty clinics.

Will MOVR 2.0 be open source? +

Yes! A core principle of MOVR 2.0 is building open source tools that benefit the entire research community. We'll be releasing data processing pipelines, analysis tools, and EMR integration components as open source projects.

When will MOVR 2.0 launch? +

We're currently in operational validation through Q2 2026. The full platform launch will depend on the success of this validation phase and sustainable funding arrangements. Stay tuned for updates as we progress through our milestones.
