What is the MOVR Data Hub?

The MOVR Data Hub represents 12+ years of neuromuscular disease research combining data from two major longitudinal studies. This unified registry provides researchers with the largest comprehensive dataset for understanding disease progression, treatment outcomes, and patient experiences across seven neuromuscular conditions.

Understanding the Data

  • Data Hub Total: Combined enrollments from both USNDR (legacy) and MOVR (active) studies
  • Clinical Encounters: Each visit where data was collected (baseline + follow-up visits)
  • Average Encounters: Indicates longitudinal data depth per participant
  • Legacy Data: USNDR study data (2013-2018) available upon request
