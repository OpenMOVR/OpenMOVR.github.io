Legacy Registry Overview

üìä The MOVR Legacy: 12+ Years of Neuromuscular Disease Research

The MOVR Datahub was a groundbreaking registry combining two major studies (USNDR 2013-2018 + MOVR 2019-2025) that collected comprehensive data on 5,895 participants across 7 neuromuscular diseases with 20,152 clinical encounters.

Quick Answer: What was MOVR?

The Neuromuscular Observational Research (MOVR) Data Hub was MDA's flagship research registry that pioneered data collection strategies to accelerate research for neuromuscular diseases. It leveraged the MDA Care Center Network to provide valuable insights into disease progression and served as a rich source of real-world evidence for researchers, clinicians, and drug developers.

MOVR's Lasting Legacy

The MOVR Datahub has concluded its data collection, but its impact continues through ongoing research studies and publications. The comprehensive dataset remains available for approved research, and the lessons learned are shaping the future MOVR 2.0 platform.

Questions about MOVR legacy data or research access? Contact us at mdamovr@mdausa.org
