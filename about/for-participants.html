Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from participants about the platform transition

Q: Is my existing data safe during this transition?

A: Yes. All existing participant data remains secure and protected under the same privacy agreements and security protocols. No changes are being made to how your current data is stored or accessed.

Q: Will I need to do anything different at my clinical visits?

A: Not immediately. Current clinical visits will continue as usual. If your site participates in the pilot program, they will inform you about any changes and get your consent before implementing new procedures.

Q: How will the new platform benefit research?

A: The automated EMR integration will allow more frequent data updates, reduce manual entry errors, and enable larger-scale studies. This should accelerate research and provide more comprehensive insights into neuromuscular diseases.

Q: Can I withdraw my participation at any time?

A: Yes. You maintain the same rights to withdraw from future data collection at any time. Your clinical care will not be affected by your participation decision.

Q: When will the new platform be ready?

A: We're currently in vendor selection and proof-of-concept phase (2024-2025). Pilot testing is planned for 2025-2026, with a go/no-go decision in March 2026 based on pilot results.

Q: How can I get involved in platform development?

A: We're planning a participant advisory committee and early access testing program. Email us at mdamovr@mdausa.org to express interest in these opportunities.
