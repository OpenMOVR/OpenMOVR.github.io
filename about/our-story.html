MOVR Evolution Timeline

Key milestones in our journey from concept to global registry platform

2013

The Beginning

Registry Conception

Started as a registry for a single disease with manual data collection processes. The vision: create a comprehensive database to accelerate neuromuscular disease research.

2015-2017

Early Growth

Expanding Disease Coverage

Extended beyond single disease to cover multiple neuromuscular conditions. Began building relationships with clinical sites and establishing data standards.

2018-2020

Scaling Challenges

Manual Process Limitations

Reached limits of manual data collection. Sites struggling with time-intensive processes. Recognized need for innovation in data capture methods.

2021-2022

Innovation Phase

Technology Exploration

Explored EMR integration possibilities, FHIR standards, and automated data capture. Started envisioning next-generation registry platform.

2023

Open Source Vision

Community-Driven Approach

Decision to pursue open source development model. Committed to building tools that benefit the entire neuromuscular disease research community.

2024-2025

Next Generation

Vendor Selection & Proof of Concept

Currently evaluating EMR integration partners and building proof-of-concept systems. Open source development planning and community building.

2026+

Future Vision

Automated EMR Integration

Vision: Replace manual data entry with automated EMR integration while maintaining gold standard data quality. Open source tools for global community.

  • Automated data capture from EMRs
  • Real-time data validation and feedback
  • Minimal additional training requirements
