üî¨ 12 Years of Learning

What We've Experienced

  • Manual Process Limitations: Seen firsthand how time-intensive data collection limits participation
  • Site Challenges: Understand the real workflow pressures clinical sites face
  • Research Barriers: Witnessed how complex processes slow scientific discovery
  • Community Needs: Learned what researchers actually need to accelerate their work

Key Insights Gained

  • Technology alone isn't enough - workflow integration is critical
  • Open source approaches democratize access and accelerate innovation
  • Patient-centered design leads to better outcomes for everyone
  • Community-driven development creates more robust solutions

  • Community Is Ready: Research community embracing open source approaches

Our Approach

  • Build on proven 12-year foundation rather than starting from scratch
  • Partner with experienced vendors rather than developing everything internally
  • Open source development to benefit entire research community
  • Transparent, work-in-progress collaboration with stakeholders
