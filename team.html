Mentorship Philosophy

Our mentors combine deep healthcare domain expertise with technical leadership in open source development. Students receive guidance on both the technical aspects of software development and the critical healthcare context that drives our mission.

Organization Context

OpenMOVR is a mission-driven extension of MDA's MOVR platform, reporting to the Chief Research Officer and focused on open source development for neuromuscular disease research.
